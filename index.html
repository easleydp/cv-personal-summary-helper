CV Personal Summary Helper

How to use this tool

Choose around 10 qualities (personal strengths) that resonate with you and best describe you professionally. This will help when it comes to writing the 'Profile' / 'Personal Summary' section of your CV.

Selected qualities appear in the panel on the right. When finished, click “Copy to clipboard” to easily paste the list into a CV editor.

Example: "A motivated and analytical professional. Adaptable in fast-paced environments, offering a dependable and conscientious approach to every challenge. Known for being articulate, meticulous, and consistently focused on delivering accurate, effective solutions with enthusiasm."
